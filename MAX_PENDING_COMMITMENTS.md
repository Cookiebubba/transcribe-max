# Max's Pending Commitments

Things **Max Blade** ([@MaxBladeTv](https://www.youtube.com/@MaxBladeTv)) has said — usually
**more than once, across multiple streams** — that he was *going to do*, but that there's no
sign of him actually finishing.

**Source:** the 56 transcribed live streams in [`transcripts/`](transcripts/) (the
"Vibe Coding Until I Buy My Family a House" series, plus the earlier 2024–25
"glowing AI commit button" streams). Quotes are pulled from YouTube's
auto-generated captions, so wording is lightly cleaned and occasionally
approximate — the day reference is the thing to trust. "Day N" = the house
series; "stream" = an older standalone broadcast.

> Scope note: this is the **kept-promising-never-finished** list. Max ships a *lot*
> day-to-day (Clippo, Canvas, the giveaways, the paid launch, dozens of small
> features). The items below are specifically the ones he raises repeatedly and
> defers repeatedly. A short "things he *did* follow through on" list is at the end
> for fairness.

## Content & channel

- **Weekly long-form YouTube videos.** The single most-repeated promise in the whole
  corpus. Framed as a course/tutorial ("complete beginner's guide to shipping your
  first app," "zero to $1,000 MRR," a free "$100,000 mastermind"). His own Hermes
  agent flagged on Day 23 that chat's #1 question is *"When are you going to post the
  vibe coding YouTube video?"* On Day 42 he even proposed a forcing function — *"if I
  don't post a video a week, I give someone $50"* — and still didn't.
- **Hire a YouTube editor / set up a real content pipeline.** Day 38: *"bring somebody
  in that helps me turn a live stream into a YouTube video… It scares the [stuff] out
  of me and that's probably why it's the route we have to go."* Repeatedly named,
  never acted on.
- **Tweet consistently ("5–10 times a day," build in public).** Days 7, 18, 28, 31,
  42. Admits he keeps slipping because streaming eats the time.
- **Launch on Product Hunt** (the commit button, then Clippo). Older streams ("a nice
  Sunday launch"), Days 7, 19, 28, 29. Never launched.
- **A live "judge the apps" segment** ("like America's Got Talent," 5-min hot-panel
  interviews about Canvas). Days 18, 28, 31, 43 — "we'll do that next week," every week.
- **Saturday / weekend / night streams.** Days 13, 14, 43 — floated repeatedly, keeps
  not doing it.
- **A studio tour.** Day 19: "at some point, but you're going to have to let me pick it
  up a little bit first."
- **The "OnlyFans" running gag.** A joke feature-vote item Max kept teasing; Day 23 his
  Hermes agent roasted him: *"You've been promising an only fans for the last 20 days
  straight."* Emblematic of the whole pattern.

## Product — Canvas (his current IDE project)

- **Ship the Canvas alpha.** The headline broken deadline: *"this weekend the alpha for
  canvas will be shipped"* (Day 31) → *"today we are going to get the alpha shipped"*
  (Day 34) → *"Our goal was to get Canvas shipped in alpha yesterday. We did not do
  that"* (Day 35).
- **The Windows port.** Day 42: *"Tomorrow I will have the working Windows version… we
  will not turn the stream off until we have a Windows version working."* Day 43: *"We're
  not working on Windows today… the Windows port is going to be more difficult than I
  thought."* (Linux port promised the same way — Days 41, 42 — also undone.)
- **The blur / smoked-glass background effect.** Burned ~4.5 hours on Day 39
  (*"after the stream, I'll spend 20 hours making sure it's got the blur effect"*),
  gave up; Day 41: *"we're going to figure out how to add blur, but not this session."*
- **Spotify / Apple Music integration.** Most-upvoted user request. Day 42 ("five
  people asking"), Day 43 (declares it impossible, then reverses: "we're gonna figure
  it out"), Day 45 still unbuilt.
- **The Canvas Community feature.** Built a v1 then kept hiding it: Day 41 "not ready to
  be shown," Day 42 "two more days of work," Day 43 still in backlog.
- **The iOS / iPad app.** Day 43: *"I need to build the iOS app… this week is going to
  be starting off the alpha of the iOS app."* Not started. (Plus an in-canvas iOS
  simulator node, behind a debug flag, never working on stream.)
- **Improve onboarding** (so new users aren't dropped onto a blank canvas). Streams
  Ris3X, Days 42, 45 — repeatedly named, not done.
- **A long tail of "we have to add this" Canvas features**, each said once or twice and
  left: a posture checker, browser-resolution simulator, Chrome-extensions browser
  node, image-gen results auto-dropping onto the canvas, Discord Rich Presence,
  drawing-tablet support, a live stock graph, loop-native execution
  (*"we will do it"* — then *"I just don't get the loop thing"*), and a Sublime/Limelight
  code editor written in Swift.
